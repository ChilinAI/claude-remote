Features

Everything you need for remote AI-powered coding

📱

Mobile Access

Full chat with Claude Code right from your iPhone or Android. Responsive design, comfortable on any screen.

⚡

Instant Delivery

Firebase Realtime Database — messages are delivered instantly. Response streaming in real time.

🔒

End-to-End Encrypted

All messages are encrypted in your browser before being sent and decrypted only on your Mac. ECDH P-256 key exchange + AES-256-GCM — even the server never sees your data in plain text.

Lightweight Tray App

Minimal footprint — just an icon in your menu bar. Auto-start, auto-update, live status indicator.

Installation

macOS may show a warning for apps downloaded outside the App Store

1

Open the DMG

Double-click the downloaded .dmg file and drag Claude Remote to your Applications folder.

2

Allow in Settings

Try to open the app — macOS will block it. Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll down and click Open Anyway. Enter your password.

3

Done!

After that, the app opens normally every time. macOS remembers your choice — no extra steps needed.
