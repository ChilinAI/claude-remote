Installation

macOS may show a warning for apps downloaded outside the App Store

1

Open the DMG

Double-click the downloaded .dmg file and drag Claude Remote to your Applications folder.

2

Allow in Settings

Try to open the app — macOS will block it. Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll down and click Open Anyway. Enter your password.

3

Done!

After that, the app opens normally every time. macOS remembers your choice — no extra steps needed.
